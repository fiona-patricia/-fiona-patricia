About Me

Hi there, I am Fiona Patricia Darma, an ambitious SMAK One Penabur student. I was born in Jakarta on September 8, 2004. Fueled by passion, I did my best to expand my knowledge and experience in this field by taking a course in design, being a part of my school’s annual event as the committee of the photography competition, and in other school's events as a design competition participant.
